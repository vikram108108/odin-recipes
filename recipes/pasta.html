Pasta!

Easy to make, Only takes 10 minutes of your time and you have a delicous lunch or dinner!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Vinigar

Steps

  1. Boil your pasta for 5-10 minutes (Can vary depending on the kind)
  2. Once boiled, drain and rinse.
  3. Add tomato sauce and cheese
  4. drop a little bit of vinigar on the top!
