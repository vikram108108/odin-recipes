Guacamole

Description

This fresh green Guacamole will go PERFECT with any bowl of chips or crackers!

Ingredients

  • Organic Avocados
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Aminos (For a more natural saltyness)
  • Tomatos
  1. Smash the avacaodos in a bowl
  2. Cube tomatos and throw in the mix
  3. Add desired seasoning
  4. Spread on bread or use with your favorite chips or crackers!
