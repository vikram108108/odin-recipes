Pancakes!

Description

These pancakes make the most LIGHT but
SATISFYING breakfast!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients (Flour, Baking Soda, and Sugar)
  2. Mix the wet Ingredients (Eggs and Milk)
  3. Stir them together until there are just a small ammout on clumps left
  4. Preheat the pan
  5. Add oil to the pan and fry them!
